How to play ?


Phase 1

Matching the artifacts.

There are total of four artifacts in each country:
1.Monument
2.National Flag
3.Currency
4.National Animal

These artifacts are designated with a moving number on the side of the image this moveable number goes in to the appropriate circle in the map once all the numbers are in the map circles we can click on the blue button below to place it if the answers are correct the user will be proceeding to next country or level if the answer is wrong the player goes back to main screen after system shows which once are wrong and he/she needs to play the same country or level again.

Monument     :    1    This number goes where that particular monument is located
National Flag :    2    This number goes where the capital of the country located.        
                                  in  the country.
Currency        :    3    This number goes where the currency is made in the country.
Animal            :    4    This number goes where that particular kind of bird or animal is 
                                  mostly found in the country.

For each country the player will be unlocking a free flight ticket to that country after finishing the level.


Phase 2

Matching the countries.

If the players finishes the first phase of the game then the player proceeds to the second phase of the game, in the second phase the player needs to remember all the flags, monuments, national birds or animals and national flags which he/she placed properly in the first phase.

In this phase of the game players are given with country names on one side and depending on the level national flags, monuments, currency or national birds or animals on the other side and drop box are placed in the middle.

Players goal is to match the artifact which belongs to that particular country and then after matching all the artifacts the player needs to press match it if the answer is correct the player gets a gold star and if the answer is wrong player gets a red star and he/she needs to repeat the level again and if he/she matches all the artifact correctly they will proceed to next level. When the user get all the 20 stars he/she will be earning a charted flight.
